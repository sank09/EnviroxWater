Operation & Maintenance of STP, ETP, WTP Plants

As a reliable partner for your water management needs, we are dedicated to providing an all-inclusive O&M service for different types of water and wastewater treatment plants, including ETP, STP, WTP, DM, and RO systems.

Our responsible crew continuously monitors your plants, performing preventive maintenance and offering rapid emergency support to guarantee smooth, uninterrupted operations. We provide constant operation management, round-the-clock (24/7), to accelerate plant processes and minimize downtime.

With over a decade of expertise, our adaptable contracts deliver highly economical and energy-saving services for industrial, commercial, and institutional sectors. We strictly adhere to the guidelines set by the Maharashtra Pollution Control Board (MPCB) and the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB).
